Internship Program at PartnerDesk

Explore diverse opportunities to grow professionally and personally.

Why Intern With Us?

PartnerDesk offers internships across multiple domains to provide students with hands-on, real-world experience. Our goal is to empower youth through skill-building and direct market exposure.

Internship Domains

  • Sales and Marketing
  • Telecalling and Client Interaction
  • Graphic Design & Video Editing
  • Web Development
  • AI/ML Based Project Development

Benefits You Get

  • Work on real business ideas and live projects
  • Earn performance-based stipends
  • Enhance your professional network
  • Improve your portfolio with startup-level exposure

Who Can Apply?

Any student currently enrolled in a college or university who has the curiosity and drive to learn by doing.

Ready to Join?

We’re excited to see you on board. Internship opportunities are open year-round. Just stay tuned to our Important Updates section on the homepage or follow us on social media to never miss out.
